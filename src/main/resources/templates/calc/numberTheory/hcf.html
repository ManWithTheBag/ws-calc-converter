highest common factor

HCF or Highest Common Factor is the greatest number which divides each of the two or more numbers. HCF is also called the Greatest Common Measure (GCM) and Greatest Common Divisor(GCD)

examples
find the HCF of numbers 450 and 390.
let's represent numbers as the product of their prime factors, for it use factoring a number into prime factors
450 = 2 · 3 · 3 · 5 · 5 390 = 2 · 3 · 5 · 13
we see that the common factors are 2, 3 and 5. then HCF(450, 390) = 2 · 3 · 5 = 30.
